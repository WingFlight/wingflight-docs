# Failsafe

The Failsafe tab configures what the aircraft does when radio link is lost:
which stage(s) to trigger, throttle/surface behavior during failsafe, and
(if GPS is fitted) whether to trigger GPS Rescue.

Configure and bench-test failsafe behavior *before* flying -- turn off your
transmitter at a safe distance from the aircraft (props off) and confirm the
flight controller actually enters failsafe and behaves the way you
configured it here.

See also: [GPS Rescue](../../flight-modes/gps-rescue.md).
